RESEARCH EXPERIENCE  
Computational Catalysis  
High-throughput screening of acidic OER catalysts with high activity and  
stability using machine learning.  
Developed a micro-kinetic model to investigate the H2 oxidation reaction  
mechanism on Pt 3 grain boundary (GB).  
Conducted Computational screening of metal GB-based catalysts for the  
Hydrogen Evolution Reaction (HER).  
Investigated the reaction pathways and mechanism of H2 oxidation on the  
Pd/PdOx interface.  
Electron Transport Calculation  
Proposed a first-principles workflow to calculate the electrical conductivity of  
2D materials under varying gas concentrations.  
Revealed the roles of carrier concentration and mobility in determining  
changes in the electrical conductivity of 2D materials when exposed to gases.  
Machine Learning  
Applied active learning strategies to develop a machine learning model for  
accurately predicting the formation energy of PbxCd1-xSe alloys.  
Utilized the Metropolis Monte Carlo method to accelerate the identification of  
the most stable PbxCd1-xSe configuration for each Pb ratio, based on the  
converged machine learning model.  
Using the fine-tune universal machine learning potential to investigate the  
cation effects on alkaline HER at Pt GB.
